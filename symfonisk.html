The story behind the product

The magic of SYMFONISK

When music meets light, something extraordinary happens. Created in collaboration with Sonos, and designed by Iina Vuorivirta, the SYMFONISK speaker lamp harnesses this power to transform how we feel at home.

Hej, Iina!

Inspired by how the flickers and crackles of a fire can change a space's atmosphere and bring people closer, Iina's design of the SYMFONISK speaker lamp was all about creating the same combination of warm ambience and immersive sound.

"SYMFONISK is all about creating an atmosphere. Sound together with light, they're almost like two sides of the same coin." – Iina Vuorivirta
